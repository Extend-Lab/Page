Break boundaries. Unlock possibilities.

Extend Lab advances intelligence in the physical world through reinforcement learning, multi-agent systems, embodied AI, and cyber-physical intelligence.

We treat reinforcement learning not only as a methodology, but also as a mindset: curious, iterative, and resilient. Our work connects theory, systems, and real-world deployment.

Research Directions

Selected themes that currently shape the lab's agenda.

Multi-Agent Systems

Collaborative and competitive decision-making for complex physical-world tasks.

Embodied AI

Agents that perceive, act, and adapt in uncertain and dynamic environments.

Simulation to Reality

Robust policy transfer from digital environments to real cyber-physical systems.

Edge Intelligence and Wireless Systems

Learning-enabled communication, resource allocation, and intelligent networking.
