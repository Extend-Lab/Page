About the Lab

Extend Lab

A lab built on the belief that boundaries are meant to be broken and possibilities are meant to be expanded.

Based at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (Guangzhou), Extend Lab pursues intelligence in the physical world through reinforcement learning, multi-agent systems, embodied AI, edge intelligence, and cyber-physical systems.

Mission

We aim to advance intelligent decision-making in complex environments and to translate that progress into robust systems for the real world. We see today's AI transformation as an opportunity to rethink how machines and humans can learn, coordinate, and act together.

Values

Curiosity, execution power, and resilience shape how we approach research. We value rigorous theory, practical experimentation, and a willingness to keep iterating until ideas become useful systems.

What We Focus On

Current directions adapted from your lab introduction materials.

Multi-Agent Systems

Collaborative and competitive intelligence for distributed physical-world tasks.

Embodied AI

Agents that sense, act, and adapt under uncertainty in dynamic environments.

Simulation and Reality

Bridging virtual training environments and real deployment with robust learning pipelines.

Edge and Wireless Intelligence

Learning-driven resource allocation, networking, and intelligent communication systems.

We welcome researchers, students, and collaborators who are excited by ambitious questions, fast iteration, and the challenge of building intelligence that works beyond the lab.
