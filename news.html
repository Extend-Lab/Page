Prof. Jiadong Yu Delivered a Forum Talk at the HKUST(GZ)-Diandong Joint Laboratory Event

In the featured report, Prof. Jiadong Yu introduced his group's work on scheduling heterogeneous computing resources for data centers, guided by the vision of making compute as accessible and on-demand as electricity. The team applies reinforcement learning to sequential decision-making in heterogeneous, highly dynamic, and resource-constrained edge-end collaborative systems, jointly optimizing communication, computation, and broader multi-objective trade-offs.
