OpenClaw and AI Tooling Sharing Session

On March 20, 2026, we organized an EXTEND Sharing Session featuring Dr. Jiadong Yu and Jiale Li. Dr. Yu introduced the fundamentals of AI agents and OpenClaw, while Jiale shared practical experiences using AI tools such as Claude Code. The session focused on OpenClaw basics, hands-on usage, and reflections on integrating AI tools into day-to-day research workflows.
